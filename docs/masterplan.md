# SyncCut AI — Master Plan

## 30-Second Elevator Pitch

SyncCut AI is an AI-powered video editing tool that automatically synchronizes music to video edits.

Users upload a video and a music track. The system analyzes scene cuts, motion intensity, and pacing, then aligns beats and drops to visual moments to create cinematic timing automatically.

Creators get perfectly timed music edits in seconds instead of manually syncing audio in editing software.

---

# Problem & Mission

## Problem

Synchronizing music to video is tedious and time-consuming.

Editors must:

- scrub timelines repeatedly
- manually match beats
- trim music awkwardly
- re-time clips

Even experienced editors can spend **10–30 minutes** syncing music for short videos.

---

## Mission

Make cinematic music timing automatic.

Core workflow:

Upload video → Upload music → AI analyzes → Automatic sync → Export video.

The goal is to eliminate manual beat matching while keeping creators in control.
